Does LEL only deal in laboratory equipment and products?


Lab Equip Ltd. is dealing in products from Health, Education & Research sectors.
The company was named Lab Equip. Ltd because when the company was formed in 1986, it started as company supplying Laboratory Equipment and Chemicals only.
Over the years the company has diversified in other areas, but we carry the same name as it has become a very well-known brand.

When was the company founded and what is your experience?


The Company was founded in 1986 in Dar es salaam, Tanzania by the Chairman of the group.
The group has been serving the industry since 1959. So LEL is benefited by the vast experience of more than six decades of its directors.

What is the major activity of the company?


LEL is the leading importer, stockiest, wholesaler and distributor of quality Health, Education and Research products in East Africa.
We offer one stop solution for all the needs of our customers. We provide pre sales, sales and after sales service all under one roof.

What is the total range of products that LEL carries and where are they being sourced?


LEL has an inventory of 5,000 distinct products which includes Laboratory Glassware, Plastic ware, Laboratory Chemicals & Reagents, Educational Instruments and Apparatus, Hospital Furniture, Medical Equipment, Disposables, Consumables, Analytical Instruments etc.

We represents more than 30 leading manufacturers from all over the world including those from North America, Europe, Japan, Far East etc.

What type of sales network does Lab Equip Ltd. have?


The corporate regional head quarter of Lab Equip Ltd. is based at Lab House, Keko Mwanga, Off Nyrere Road, Dar es salaam.
We also have showroom in the city centre at Zaramo Street, Opp. Channel Ten, DTV Roundabout.
Lastly we have a branch office in the beautiful island of Zanzibar. Apart from these sales outlets, we have our sales and marketing team spread all over Tanzania and East Africa.

How is Lab Equip Ltd. different from its other competitors?


Lab Equip Ltd, is a pioneer in Health, Education and Research sectors. Our 34 years of on field experience is unmatched with any of our competitors.

Also the wide range of products that we carry distinguishes LEL from the others in the market. The quality of our product, competitive prices and excellent after sales support are the 3 key factors which differentiates LEL from the rest.
